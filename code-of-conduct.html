Code of Conduct The Ohlone Club Water Polo is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment for all members, coaches, officials, and spectators. We expect all individuals involved with the club to: Conduct themselves with integrity, sportsmanship, and respect for others Adhere to USA Water Polo rules and regulations Refrain from behavior that may be considered harassment, bullying, or discriminatory Respect the rights and dignity of fellow athletes, coaches, officials, and spectators Comply with club policies and procedures Maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information with discretion By participating in Ohlone Club Water Polo activities, members acknowledge their understanding and commitment to upholding this Code of Conduct. Consequences of Non-Compliance Failure to adhere to this Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action, including but not limited to: Verbal warning Written warning Suspension from team activities Expulsion from the club Reporting Incidents If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to a coach, team administrator, or club official.
